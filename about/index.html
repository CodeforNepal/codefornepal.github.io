About Code for Nepal

A community of volunteers using civic technology to increase digital literacy and harness the power of open data to build a more transparent, equitable Nepal.

Who we are

Our Mission

Code for Nepal is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit working to increase digital literacy and use of open data across Nepal. We believe technology is a tool for equity — and that Nepalis everywhere should have access to the digital skills and resources to shape their own futures.

Founded in 2014, we have grown from a small volunteer group to a community of 500+ members across Nepal and the Nepali diaspora. We build open-source tools, run training fellowships, and advocate for data-driven public services.

Our values

What We Stand For

  • Open & Transparent

    Our code, data, and processes are open by default. We believe in public accountability.

  • Community First

    Every decision we make centers on the needs of the communities we serve.

  • Inclusive & Equitable

    We actively work to remove barriers to digital access, especially for women and underserved communities.

  • Impact-Driven

    We measure success by real outcomes: skills learned, projects delivered, communities empowered.

Focus areas

What We Do

Digital Literacy

Expanding digital skills through structured workshops and online programs, reaching learners from all backgrounds across Nepal.

Open Data

Making Nepal's civic and government data accessible and understandable — building tools like NepalMap to help citizens engage with public information.

Fellowships & Training

Running the Data Fellowship program to train the next generation of Nepali data scientists and civic technologists through free, expert-led education.

Community

Connecting 500+ volunteers across Nepal and the diaspora to collaborate, create impact, and advocate for a more digital, data-informed Nepal.

How it works

How We Operate

Volunteers Power Us

Code for Nepal runs entirely on volunteer effort. Our global community of developers, designers, and data scientists give their time to create public goods.

We Build in the Open

All our projects are open-source. Anyone can contribute, fork, and build on our work. Transparency is core to everything we create.

Impact Drives Direction

We prioritize projects and programs where we can demonstrate measurable real-world impact — skills trained, data made accessible, communities engaged.
