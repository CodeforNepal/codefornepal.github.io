Join Us

You can help us and many in Nepal just from your couch or sofa. You can be a designer, marketer, coder, writer, problem-solver, or anyone else who is interested in helping people make better use of technology, become digitally and data literate. If that sounds like you, join us via:

Slack (Say hello and introduce yourself) Github (You can start looking at projects and contribute by resolving issues or suggesting enhancements) Facebook Twitter

We also encourage you to explore our website, learn about our projects, and read the “Code of Conduct” policy.
