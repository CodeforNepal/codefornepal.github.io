Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?
Any Nepali is welcome to apply — whether you're a student, working professional, or someone looking to change careers. No prior data experience is required; we welcome complete beginners.
Is the fellowship really free?
Yes! The Data Fellowship is completely free, thanks to DataCamp Donates. Fellows receive full access to DataCamp's premium platform at no cost.
How long is the fellowship?
The fellowship is self-paced. Fellows are expected to complete at least one DataCamp track within a few months. Most fellows complete the program within 3–6 months.
What data skills can I learn?
DataCamp offers courses across Python, R, SQL, data visualization, machine learning, AI, data engineering, and business analytics. You choose the track that best fits your goals.
Do I need to be in Nepal to participate?
No! The fellowship is fully online and open to Nepalis worldwide — whether you're in Nepal or part of the diaspora abroad. All you need is internet access.

Ready to apply?
