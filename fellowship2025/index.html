Data Fellowship 2025

We are pleased to announce that applications are open for Code For Nepal Data Fellowship. The objective of this fellowship organized by Code for Nepal with support from DataCamp Donates is to help anyone, especially those who cannot afford DataCamp subscription, build their data skills and make progress in their lives.

Applications open: April 15, 2025 (Rolling basis)

We are grateful to DataCamp for donating access to their expert-led data skill training. DataCamp offers online training for all data skills and levels, from non-coding essentials for business to data science and machine learning, all taught by leading experts in the field. DataCamp is used by 1,600+ companies such as Google, Uber, PayPal and others to build skills of their employees.

Every Nepali interested in learning new skills can apply. Apply using the form below!

About the program

The Data Fellowship includes:
  1. A DataCamp Premium subscription to take courses and earn certificates.
  2. Opportunity to publish data visualizations and blog posts via Code for Nepal site. (When data visualizations and or blog posts of fellows who have earned DataCamp certificate will be selected to be published by Code for Nepal.)
  3. Industry/academia expert teaching assistants (offline and online) to support learning, community participation.
  4. Involvement in Open Source Projects.
  5. A digital certificate from Code for Nepal after the completion of the fellowship.
  6. Opportunity to contribute to amazing open source data projects after completion of the program.
  7. Opportunity be part of Code For Nepal Community.

Fellowship components

Step 1: DataCamp Courses

Fellows should complete the DataCamp course in the given timeframe to enhance their data and digital skills. (Every fellow has 3 months to complete their DataCamp course from the date they get access to DataCamp.)

Step 2: Assignment

Fellows should submit the assignment in the form of fellowship-section blog post, data visualization in any form of publishable output.

Step 3: Support

Fellows get an opportunity to further work on the organization project, recommendation of the job and internship and other support opportunities.


Learning outcomes of the fellowship

  1. Community-based learning
  2. Involvement in Data-oriented project
  3. Mentorship sessions (online) via Slack
  4. Preparation for Data Analyst Associate, Data Engineer Associate and Data Scientist Associate Certifications

Application Details

Application Open: April 15, 2025
Deadline to Apply: Rolling basis
Number of seats: Limited

Applicants must meet the following criteria to apply

  1. Able to complete the program
  2. Must be at least 16 years of age
  3. Background in basic programming

We will call for interviews if you are shortlisted. The selected fellows must complete all the phases of the program to receive certification.

You only have to fill one application form.
